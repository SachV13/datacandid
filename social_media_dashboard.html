Before ChatGPT (Nov 2022), AI-generated social content was near zero. In just 2 years, AI posts on Facebook jumped 670%+. 71% of all social media images are now AI-generated. Only 3.6% of social media professionals don't use AI in their workflow.

Organic Engagement Collapse
As algorithms became more complex and AI flooded feeds with content, real organic human engagement plummeted. Facebook's organic reach dropped 98% in 13 years — from 16% of followers in 2012 to 0.2% in 2025. TikTok started strong but is now declining too.

Authenticity & Consumer Trust
Public trust in AI companies has dropped 8 points since 2021. Nearly 60% of consumers now doubt the authenticity of online content. Over half reduce their engagement when they suspect content is AI-generated.

Algorithm Evolution Timeline
From Facebook's controversial News Feed launch in 2006 to TikTok's AI-native For You Page in 2016 to today's behavioural surveillance systems — the algorithm has become the most powerful editorial force in human history.
Key algorithm milestones
2006
Facebook News Feed launches
First ever ML-curated social feed. Massive user backlash within 24 hours — hundreds of thousands protest.
2009
Personalised ranking begins
Facebook moves away from chronological posts. Relevance scores introduced.
2011
Feed becomes fully algorithmic
Chronological Facebook effectively ends. Instagram hashtags launch.
2015
Time-on-platform optimisation
Facebook shifts from social sharing to maximising passive scroll time. Personal posting begins to decline.
2016
Instagram kills chronological feed
Massive backlash. TikTok launches with the world's first fully AI-native social feed — no social graph needed.
2018
Outrage engine: angry emoji = 5× a like
Facebook's algorithm rewards emotional reactions. Inflammatory content spreads faster than ever.
2021
Instagram copies TikTok — launches Reels
Photo-first platform becomes video-first. FYP model replaces social graph.
2022
ChatGPT launches (Nov 30)
AI-generated content becomes mass-accessible. Social media AI post rates begin explosive climb.
2023
TikTok labels 1.3B AI videos
Platforms begin acknowledging the AI content flood. Mandatory labeling discussions begin.
2025
Facebook "Andromeda" — behavioural surveillance
AI predicts behaviour based on scrolling patterns, pauses, hesitations. Engagement metrics deepen to physiological level.
2025
X (Twitter) pay-to-amplify era
Premium subscribers get 4× visibility boost. Reach officially becomes a paid product.
2026
Shift to "satisfaction metrics"
Platforms begin optimising for perceived quality over raw engagement — acknowledging 15 years of optimising the wrong thing.
